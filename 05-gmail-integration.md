# Gmail Integration: Read and Manage Email with AI Help

Connect Gmail in MosAIc to triage inboxes faster, summarize messages, and draft replies more confidently.

## What this feature helps with

- Summarizing long email threads.
- Turning inbox items into action lists.
- Drafting clearer responses.

## 1) Connect your Gmail account

1. Open **Gmail** from the app navigation.
2. Use the connect flow to sign in and authorize access.
3. Return to MosAIc and confirm your account is connected.

Use the account you actually manage daily to get immediate value.

## 2) Start with inbox triage

Run a simple routine:

- Review recent important emails.
- Ask for summaries in plain language.
- Convert emails into a short to-do list.

This is usually the fastest way to save time on day one.

## 3) Draft replies with your tone

When drafting, tell the assistant:

- Who the reply is for.
- The tone you want (formal, friendly, concise).
- The main decision or request.

Always do a final human review before sending.

## 4) Keep email usage safe

- Use least-access settings whenever possible.
- Revoke access if you stop using the integration.
- Avoid sharing sensitive details in prompts unless necessary.

## A practical daily flow

1. Summarize new important emails.
2. Generate top priorities.
3. Draft responses for the top 3 messages.
4. Edit and send manually.
